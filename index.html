Ministries

Men's Ministry

A fellowship of men committed to growing in faith and building strong relationships. We gather for occasional events like breakfasts, service projects, and special outings that encourage spiritual growth and community. Join us as we walk together in faith and grow as brothers in Christ.

Pine Manor

Pine Manor Ministry is dedicated to bringing love and hope to the residents of Pine Manor. Every Sunday, we joyfully transport residents to our church for worship, creating a space where they can connect and feel part of our church family. Additionally, we gather with them once a month at Pine Manor for fellowship, prayer, and encouragement. This ministry is a beautiful opportunity to share God’s love and build meaningful connections with our senior community.

Women of Purpose

A community of women committed to growing in faith, building authentic relationships, and serving with purpose. Through Bible studies, fellowship events, and outreach opportunities, we aim to empower and inspire women of all ages to live out their God-given potential. Join us as we support each other, deepen our faith, and make a meaningful impact in our church and community.

Lead Pastor

Pastor Benny has faithfully led York Calvary Temple Assembly of God for over 20 years, serving with a heart dedicated to God and His people. Alongside his wife, Joyce, he has passionately guided our church family, sharing the love of Christ through his preaching, teaching, and pastoral care. Pastor Benny is known for his compassionate spirit, commitment to God’s Word, and desire to see lives transformed by the power of the Gospel. Together, he and Joyce are deeply invested in the church and community, continuously encouraging and equipping others to grow in their faith.
